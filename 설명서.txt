Q n A

How do I back up my Shipworld File?
==============================
1. Play Starbound with the character's ship you want to back up.
2. Close Starbound.
3. Right–click Starbound in your Steam library.
4. Click properties -> Local Files Tab -> Browse Local Files
5. Open the following folders -> Storage -> player
6. Right click on the white space without selecting a file then select Sort by -> Date Modified in the dropdown menu.
7. Create a new folder.
8. Copy the top file that ends in “.shipworld” into that new folder.

How do I get the custom life pod on my save file?
==============================
Back up your ship world for the character you want to give the capsule to as described in the last section – but move the .shipworld file to the new folder instead of copying it. Make sure you get all the stuff you want to bring with you off your ship before doing this.
1. Ensure all items that you want to keep are removed from your ship.
2. Exit Starbound.
3. Delete the .shipworld file that you just made a backup for.
4. Reload Starbound and log on as your character.
5. Congratulations! You now have custom life pod!
